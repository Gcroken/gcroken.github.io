Take Action

View at Sandy Hook unit of Gateway National Recreation Area in Middletown, NJ.

Use your voice to advocate for America's National Parks!

Ask congress to reauthorize the Great American Outdoors Act and extend the life of the legislation! Help continue to combat the Park Service's maintenance backlog to ensure safe and enjoyable parks for everyone.

3 Simple Steps to Become a GAOA Advocate

Take meaningful action by contacting your local representative! It's their responsibility to listen to their constituents' concerns (that's you!) and push for legislation that supports the protection of the parks. Your voice can make a difference!

Steps Action
Find your Local Rep Click here and input your zip code to match with your local U.S. Representative.
Choose your Contact Method Your rep's email and office phone number should be easily found on their webpage. Either contact method works! Or you can set up an in-person meeting.
Craft your Message Create a script that urges your representative to do what they can to extend the Great American Outdoor Act. Make it personal by recounting your experiences at the parks!

Prefer a template? The National Park Conservation Association has created a quick and easy pre-written message to send to your representative. Check out their website at NPCA.org to learn more.
